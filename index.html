About

This is a compilation of easy-to-follow recipes that is useful for anyone seeking to make a simple meal. During the pandemic, I spent more time cooking: adapting new recipes and learning from my mom. This website started as a way to document what I learned during this time, and now it continues to serve as a resource for me to document my cooking experiences for future use. I hope this simple website also serves as a valuable resource for others who are looking for a quick, simple, and of course, delicious meal.



- Kailey Wong

Roasted Broccolini

Ingredients:

  • 1 bunch broccolini, cut into bite-size stalks
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper

Directions:

  1. Lay the stalks of broccolini out in a pan.
  2. Drizzle the olive oil over the broccolini, then sprinkle the garlic salt and pepper over the broccolini.
  3. Preheat the oven to 370ÂºF.
  4. Roast the broccolini in a pan for about 15 minutes, or until crispy.

Lemon Curd

Ingredients:

  • zest and juice of 2 lemons
  • 3 eggs, whipped
  • 6 tbsp unsalted butter
  • ¾ cup sugar

Directions:

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a mixing bowl.
  2. Place the bowl over a pan of boiling water and stir the ingredients until the mixture is smooth and thick.
  3. Pour the mixture into clean warm jars then store in the refrigerator.
