Roasted Cauliflower

Ingredients:

  • 1 head cauliflower, cut into bite-size florets
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons garlic salt
  • 2 teaspoons cayenne
  • 2 tablespoons thyme
