Roasted Cauliflower

Ingredients:

  • 1 head cauliflower, cut into bite-size florets
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons garlic salt
  • 2 teaspoons cayenne
  • 2 tablespoons thyme

Directions:

  1. Toss the ingredients together in a bowl or pan.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350ºF.
  3. Roast the cauliflower in a pan for about 15 minutes, or until golden and soft.

Roasted Broccolini

Ingredients:

  • 1 bunch broccolini, cut into bite-size stalks
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper

Directions:

  1. Lay the stalks of broccolini out in a pan.
  2. Drizzle the olive oil over the broccolini, then sprinkle the garlic salt and pepper over the broccolini.
  3. Preheat the oven to 370ºF.
  4. Roast the broccolini in a pan for about 15 minutes, or until crispy.
