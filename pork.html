Pork Recipes


Baked Chinese-Vietnamese Pork Chops

Ingredients:

  • 6 thin, center-cut pork chops
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • ½ teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon five-spice powder
  • 5 tablespoons brown sugar
  • ½ teaspoon ginger, minced
  • 1 teaspoon shallot, minced
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ onion, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Directions:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Allow the pork chops to marinate in the fridge for as long as possible.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350ºF.
  3. Place the pork chops in a pan and bake for 20 to 30 minutes.

Shake and Bake Pork Chops

Ingredients:

  • four ¾-inch bone-in pork chops
  • 1½ cups panko
  • 5 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 tablespoons grated parmesan
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ teaspoon pepper

Directions:

  1. Combine all ingredients excluding the pork chops into a large resealable plastic bag. Coat the pork chops with water. One at a time, place the pork chops in the bag and shake well to coat. Press the mixture firmly into the pork chops.
  2. Preheat the oven to 375ºF.
  3. Coat a pan with oil or procure a baking sheet on a wire rack. Place the pork chops on the pan or rack and coat them with any remaining breadcrumbs. Bake for about 30 minutes until the breadcrumbs are a dark golden color.

Asian Pork Tenderloins

Ingredients:

  • one 2-pound pork tenderloin, trimmed of fat
  • ⅓ cup light soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 6 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • ¼ cup sesame oil

Directions:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Allow the pork to marinate in the fridge for as long as possible.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350ºF.
  3. Place the pork chops in a pan and bake for 20 to 30 minutes.

Asian Marinated Pork Chops

Ingredients:

  • 8 thin, center-cut pork chops

  2. Preheat the oven to 350ºF.
  3. Place the pork chops in a pan and bake for 20 to 30 minutes.

Honey Garlic Pork Chops

Ingredients:

  • 6 thin, center-cut pork chops
  • juice of 2 medium-sized lemons
  • ¼ cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive or sesame oil

Directions:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Allow the pork chops to marinate in the fridge for as long as possible.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350ºF.
  3. Place the pork chops in a pan and bake for 20 to 30 minutes.

Ketchup Pork Chops

Ingredients:

  • 6 thin, center-cut pork chops
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • juice of ½ lemon
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Directions:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl then pour over the pork chops in a baking pan.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350ºF.
  3. Bake for 30 minutes.

Thai Pork Chops

Ingredients:

  • 6 thin, center-cut pork chops
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • juice of 1 lime
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Directions:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Allow the pork chops to marinate in the fridge for 30 minutes or more.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350ºF.
  3. Place the pork chops in a pan and bake for 20 to 30 minutes.
